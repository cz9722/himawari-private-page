page two

RANDOM SHIT

a few thoughts

Sometimes I want to keep my distance from people,

so I do not have to pretend,

so I can live on without showing fake care,

and simply be myself.

I never thought that would change.

Then you arrived,

and somehow your presence

could make the room feel lighter.

In my everyday life—

smiling eyes,

a "good morning",

a small talk—

those would make my day.

page three

Little fragments

Click them if you want. They open one by one.

fragment I

Feelings

Frankly, it was odd to me

that one person can alter the air like this.

Maybe it does not make sense.

Maybe it does not have to...

fragment II

Impression

At first, I was drawn by this weird sense of familiarity of you.

Then I realized-

I want more than just knowing you.

I want to be with you.

fragment III

Questions

Every time I see you,

the questions I meant to ask slip away from my head.

...

What have you gone through in your life,

and what have you done with it?

What is your favorite color?

What brings you joy?

What makes your anger rise?

What thoughts keep you awake at night?

And when I appear in your mind,

what do you think of?

What is it like to walk with you in the rain?

...

fragment IV

Seize the moment...

The questions that

I wanna ask seem endless.

But I don't know

where to begin,

or how to ask at all.

Because one of my traits

is that I always seek for

the perfect timing to do things.

fragment V

My phobia

Maybe I was afraid

of fucking up our relationship

since the second I recognized my feelings.

Maybe that is why

Sometimes I suddenly became shy.

And yet, the more I learn about you,

the more comfortable I feel

when I'm around you.

fragment VI

Dreams

Since the new year,

you have been appearing in my dreams

more and more often.

Sometimes you seem sad there,

and I do what I can

to make you smile.

You remember the dream of valley that

you were in?

First colorful dream for me!

last page

I like philosophy shit.

Tried to make this part a poem but...

It may sound simple, even cliché

but I wish you could be happy

I wish I could drive away your gloom

the way you drove away mine

You once asked me

why do people go after love

I still do not have an answer

and I do not think it should have one

But maybe for me

it is this

when I am with you

or when you are in my thoughts

I feel happier

I feel more alive

I wonder around

We are like dancers in a midnight tango

each with our own steps

our own rhythm

our own hesitation

You showed me your pace

I'll try to follow

I’ve always wanted to thank you

but chinese always make the scene feel awkward

What I mean is thank you for coming into my life

quietly lighting up the dull and heavy days I once had

I hope we can create more memories in the future

　

(PS: I don’t mean to be very dramatic about this, if you find it that way…)
